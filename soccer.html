Play it Forward

A half-day soccer tournament hosted by Daniels Corporation and sponsored by Speedy Electrical Contractors Ltd., in support of Youth Empowering Parents

Register for the 2020 Tournament

Contact Us

When:

Thursday, September 20, 2020*
(Date to be TBD) 3:00PM - 6:00PM

Cost:

$1,000; $2,000 for event sponsorship

Where:

Regent Park Athletic Grounds, 480 Shuter Street, Toronto, ON

Included:

- Guarantee play in 5 games, 7 minutes each
- 5-10 players / team (5v5, unlimited substitutions)
- Branded t-shirts for each player & team photos
- Awesome awards and prizes

On Friday, September 27th, 2019, Daniels Corporation hosted Play it Forward, a soccer tournament in support of Youth Empowering Parents. This half-day soccer tournament brought together organizations involved in architecture and trades, and raised money to create meaningful leadership opportunities for more than 150 at-risk children and youth.
