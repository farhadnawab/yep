The Tutor Training Hub

Here, you'll learn how to become a good tutor to a parent or another family member at home

Here's how it works

First, you'll learn how to
become a tutor

It only takes about 15 minutes to learn the basics

Then, you'll join a short
online discussion

You'll tell us what you'd like to teach, and you'll get tips and tricks to help you teach

Then, you'll teach a parent
or an adult you know

Check in with us weekly to tell us what you've done, so we can track your volunteer hours

Welcome!
Here's a quick introduction before you start

We've got a couple of short videos and quizzes that will help you learn the basics of becoming a tutor. The examples you'll see and read about will show a demonstration of a student attempting to teach computer skills to an adult. However, the things you'll learn are applicable no matter what you teach. You can use these teaching skill whether you teach English language skills, dance moves, a musical instrument, or just about anything else you're good at.

You can't save your progress, but it will only take you about 15 minutes to complete.
